Code Quiz Rules

  • You have 100 seconds to answer 10 multiple choice questions.
  • If you pick an incorrect choice then 5 seconds are subtracted from your remaining time.
  • You will only progress to the next question once you have selected the correct answer to the current question.
  • The game ends once you have answered all questions correctly or when time has run out.
  • The remaining time is your final score which you can choose to record with your initials.
